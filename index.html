A Note to the Visitor

You find yourself in a world woven from ink and ivy. Here, an aloof curator named Sei tends to his library, a sanctuary of perfected stories. Your unexpected arrival disrupts his quiet world, but perhaps your presence is the one story he didn't know he was missing.

This is a hopeful experience about how patience and understanding can build bridges in a disconnected world.

It gently explores themes of perfectionism, social anxiety, and the challenge of forming new connections.
